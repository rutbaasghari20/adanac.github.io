    Elevate Quality Assurance & Quality Control with Second- and Third-Party Inspection & Engineering Services

    ADANAC, in collaboration with its international partners, specializes in delivering Inspection & Engineering Consultancy services tailored to ensure products and installations meet regulatory, voluntary, or client-specific requirements in the Middle East and North America.

    Upholding quality and compliance standards are pivotal for businesses striving for excellence. However, achieving these feat demands expertise and resources. This is where ADANAC's Inspection & Engineering services prove invaluable, offering comprehensive assistance in assessing, verifying, and enhancing quality standards across diverse industries.
    Our Services:

  • Product Inspections Conducting Second and Third-party inspections for comprehensive evaluations of product quality, specifications, and adherence to industry standards.
  • Supplier Audits Assessing supplier capabilities, processes, and compliance to ensure supply chain reliability and consistency.
  • Factory Inspections Evaluating manufacturing facilities to ensure adherence to quality control procedures, safety regulations, and ethical practices.
  • Quality Management Systems (QMS) Audits Reviewing and optimizing QMS to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.
  • Document Review and Certification Validating documentation, certifications, and regulatory compliance for accuracy and validity.
    • Our Offerings:

  • Second & Third-party Inspection, including site Quality Assurance, Quality Control, Safety, and HSE services.
  • Secondment Services: Providing Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, HVAC & HSE secondment services.
  • Green Hydrogen Consultancy.
  • In-services Inspection of Lifting Equipment & gears, Earth Moving Equipment, and Pressure Systems Equipment, including Thermography.
  • Non-Destructive Testing.
  • Design Review Services.

    • Our Expertise

    We boast a team of qualified engineers and inspectors with professional qualifications in ASNT, PCN, AWS, CSWIP, BGAS, NACE, and API standards.

    Why Choose ADANAC?

    With extensive experience in Second & Third-party inspection services, our highly trained inspectors meticulously cover all stages of the production process. We ensure that potential defects, deviations, or non-compliance issues are detected early, enabling prompt corrective actions.

    As an independent third-party inspection company, we maintain complete neutrality from manufacturers and suppliers, offering unbiased and impartial assessments. Our inspections identify potential risks, allowing proactive measures to minimize product recalls, returns, or customer complaints, thereby safeguarding brand reputation and reducing financial losses.

    We have a global network of inspectors, facilitating inspection services across various regions and countries. Leveraging the latest inspection tools and technologies, we deliver accurate and efficient services, ensuring optimal outcomes for our clients.
