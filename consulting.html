    Empowering Sustainability: EGS Consulting Services

    Welcome to our platform dedicated to advancing sustainability through comprehensive EGS (Environmental, Social, and Governance) Consulting Services. In a world where environmental concerns, social responsibility, and ethical governance including Quality, Health, Safety, Environment, Information Security, Business Continuity, Risk Management and Social Responsibility practices are paramount, we offer tailored solutions to organizations seeking to navigate the complexities of sustainability and make a positive impact on the planet and society.

    At our core, we are driven by a commitment to foster sustainability in all aspects of business and society. Our mission is clear: to empower organizations to prioritize QHSE considerations, minimize risks, enhance performance, and create sustainable value for all stakeholders. Through our comprehensive consultancy services, we aim to foster a culture of continuous improvement and compliance, ultimately contributing to a safer, healthier, and more sustainable world.

    Our Services



  • QHSE Consultancy (ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001):
    • Design, Development and implementation of Quality Management Systems (QMS) based on ISO 9001 standards, Environmental Management Systems (EMS) aligned with ISO 14001 standards to manage environmental impacts, Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems (OHSMS) compliant with ISO 45001 standards to ensure workplace safety.
    • QHSE audits, assessments, and process optimization to ensure adherence to international standards, regulatory compliance, and best practices.
    • Training and capacity building programs to enhance quality awareness and competence among staff.
  • Information Security Consulting (ISO 27001):
    • Development and implementation of Information Security Management Systems (ISMS) based on ISO 27001 standards.
    • Cybersecurity risk assessments, vulnerability scans, and penetration testing to safeguard digital assets.
    • Security awareness training, policy development, and incident response planning in line with ISO 27001 requirements.
  • Business Continuity Planning (ISO 22301):
    • Business impact analysis (BIA) and risk assessments to identify critical processes and vulnerabilities.
    • Development of Business Continuity Plans (BCPs) and Disaster Recovery Plans (DRPs) based on ISO 22301 standards.
    • Tabletop exercises, simulations, and post-incident reviews to test and refine continuity strategies.
  • Social Responsibility Advisory (ISO 26000):
    • Stakeholder engagement, materiality assessments, and sustainability strategy development guided by ISO 26000 principles.
    • Corporate social responsibility (CSR) reporting, performance measurement, and benchmarking against ISO 26000 guidelines.
    • Ethical supply chain management, diversity and inclusion initiatives, and community engagement programs in alignment with ISO 26000 recommendations.
